What is the "Mail Order Service" and How Do I Take Advantage of it ?

For members who require medications for ongoin conditions, or who live or may spend time in an area where there are no participating/in-network pharmacies, "mail order service" can save money, time, and certainly any worry about getting prescriptions filled.
